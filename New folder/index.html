The site

This is my weblog and podcast of things that have interested me since MY school in 2008, Sometimes On My Notebook and sometimes making some binary files as i grew up, I never pushed them over the internet. My mobile phone notes app was filled with things like poetry, articles, quranic interpretations and commentary especially Maulana Maududi and ibni Kaseer. It started as a silly hobby, but it became a way to meet friends, be flamed, and how a cool person like yourself could cross my path. I’m grateful.
